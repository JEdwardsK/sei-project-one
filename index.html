HOW TO PLAY

the aim of the game is to prevent an invasion by destroying all of the invaders before they reach the ground. If any of them make it to the ground, or touch the player, it's game over!

To navigate across the planets surface, use either the left and right arrow keys, or alternatively the A and D keys on your keyboard

You also need to be able to fight back! press the spacebar in order to fire a specialised invader killing plasma bolt

Watch out! As they make their way towards the planet's surface, the aliens may open fire! You can doge this rain of fire, or shoot back with your own weaponry to block

We have a supply of special bombs that will kill a number of enemies in a single hit. However they are temperamental and will shooting regular bolts will reset their charge. Leep an eye on your visor for an indicator when the bombs are charged and operational. press the P key to open fire

On occasion some fighters have observed fast moving ships scouting above. If you see one, shoot it down! They sometimes carry advanced shielding tech that can protect you from a round of enemy fire!
